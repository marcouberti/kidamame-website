 Our mission

Meaningful screen time for families

We are a small independent studio, from parents to parents. We create calm, offline, and ad-free apps designed for primary school children (ages 4-10). Our goal is simple: to help families slow down and rediscover the joy of curiosity through nature and science. No noise, no addiction, just a safe space to explore together.

 Our apps

Meet Kidamame Discovery

Welcome to Kidamame Discovery, our calm, interactive world designed for families. Rooted in Montessori principles, this experience offers meaningful screen time for children ages 4-10. We replace fast-paced goals and instructions with child-led exploration, inviting kids to touch, play, and learn at their own pace. Each sensory-rich scene is an illustrated storybook come to life, where tapping an animal or plant reveals beautiful "Did You Know?" cards. It’s a magical, ad-free space for families to slow down, spark curiosity, and share moments of wonder together. Explore the world of Kidamame Discovery.

 What we stand for

Built on principles
parents care about.

We believe in curiosity, wonder, and the magic of learning something new—together. We're building apps that will create meaningful experiences to bring families closer while exploring the natural world. From the sounds of the forest to the secrets of the animal kingdom, our experiences help kids explore nature, science, and the outdoors through imaginative play. Every interaction is crafted to spark curiosity and create lasting memories.

Calm by design

No flashing lights, no urgency loops, no addictive mechanics. Just gentle, peaceful play.

Safe & offline

Truly offline. No accounts, no data collection, no ads. Just a private, independent space to explore anywhere.

Genuinely educational

Inspired by Montessori: discovery over instruction, curiosity over competition.

Beautiful craft

Every detail is thoughtfully curated. Kids and parents deserve beautiful spaces to explore.

Shared wonder

Our apps are built for quality time. They are designed for side-by-side discovery, sparking the kind of curiosity that families enjoy together.

No subscriptions

Start your journey for free. If you love it, a single purchase unlocks a world of wonder forever.
