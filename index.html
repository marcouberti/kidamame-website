Our Mission

We are a small independent studio, from parents to parents. We create calm, offline, and ad-free apps designed for primary school children (ages 4-10). Our goal is simple: to help families slow down and rediscover the joy of curiosity through nature and science. No noise, no addiction, just a safe space to explore together.

KIDAMAME DISCOVERY

Welcome to Kidamame Discovery, our calm, interactive world designed for families. Rooted in Montessori principles, this experience offers meaningful screen time for children ages 4-10. We replace fast-paced goals and instructions with child-led exploration, inviting kids to touch, play, and learn at their own pace. Each sensory-rich scene is an illustrated storybook come to life, where tapping an animal or plant reveals beautiful "Did You Know?" cards. It’s a magical, ad-free space for families to slow down, spark curiosity, and share moments of wonder together.
