Our Mission

From parents to parents

We are a small independent studio. We create calm, offline, and ad-free apps designed for primary school children (ages 4–10). Our goal is simple: to help families slow down and rediscover the joy of curiosity through nature and science. No noise, no addiction — just a safe space to explore together.

Our App

Kidamame Discovery

A calm, interactive world designed for families. Rooted in Montessori principles, offering meaningful screen time for children ages 4–10.

We replace fast-paced goals with child-led exploration. Each sensory-rich scene is an illustrated storybook come to life — tapping an animal or plant reveals beautiful “Did You Know?” cards.
