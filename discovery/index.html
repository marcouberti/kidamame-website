Kidamame Discovery

Welcome to a calm, interactive world designed for families. Kidamame Discovery replaces fast-paced goals and instructions with child-led exploration, inviting kids to touch, play, and learn at their own pace.

Pure Exploration & Wonder

Built entirely around curiosity, there are absolutely no things to win and no puzzles to solve. Kids simply pan across the landscape, explore the environment, and listen to the authentic sounds of animals and nature. Tapping the magnifying glass reveals beautifully illustrated cards filled with fascinating facts about each element.

Interactive Secrets & Surprises

Every sensory-rich scene contains about 20 interactive elements and hidden surprises. We focus on the "small wonders," showing how even the tiniest creatures or plants hide big secrets. Tap the ostrich to see its eggs fall, touch the volcano to trigger a spectacular eruption, or watch the glacier front collapse into the sea!

A Growing World

The app features 6 stunning scenes, including the Alps, the African Savannah, the Australian Outback, the Arctic, and the Amazon Rainforest. With around 120 interactive elements and 3 fun facts per element, there are over 400 interesting facts waiting to be discovered!

Quality Time Together

We designed Kidamame Discovery as a shared experience. The app shines when a child plays alongside an adult, exploring the biomes together while the parent helps reading the fun facts which, by the way, are genuinely interesting for adults too! Our ultimate aspiration is that this shared digital wonder will inspire your family to step outside and discover the magic of the real world.
