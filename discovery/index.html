Kidamame Discovery

Welcome to a calm, interactive world designed for families. Kidamame Discovery replaces fast-paced goals and instructions with child-led exploration, inviting kids to touch, play, and learn at their own pace.

Pure Exploration & Wonder

Built entirely around curiosity and Montessori principles, there are absolutely no things to win and no puzzles to solve. Kids simply pan across the landscape, explore the environment, and listen to the authentic sounds of animals and nature. Tapping the magnifying glass reveals beautifully illustrated cards filled with fascinating facts about each element.

Interactive Animals, Plants & Natural Phenomena

The app features 6 stunning scenes: the Alps, the African Savannah, the Australian Outback, the Arctic, the Sonoran Desert, and the Amazon Rainforest. Each sensory-rich biome contains 20 or more interactive elements (animals, plants, and natural phenomena). We focus on the "small wonders," showing how even the tiniest creatures hide big secrets. Tap the ostrich to see its eggs fall, touch the volcano to trigger a spectacular eruption, or watch the glacier front collapse into the sea! With over 120 interactive elements in total, there are about 400 fascinating facts waiting to be discovered!

Quality Time Together

We designed Kidamame Discovery as a shared experience. The app shines when a child plays alongside an adult, exploring the biomes together while the parent helps reading the fun facts which, by the way, are genuinely interesting for adults too! In line with the latest American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) recommendations on screen time, Kidamame is built to encourage co-viewing and meaningful family interactions. Our ultimate aspiration is that this shared digital wonder will inspire your family to step outside and discover the magic of the real world.

No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions.

We know how hard it is to find digital spaces you can trust. That's why we created The Kidamame Promise: our apps will never feature advertisements, hidden subscription fees, or confusing in-game currencies. You can try the app for free and, if your family loves it, a single one-time purchase unlocks the entire world forever. Furthermore, we collect absolutely no personal data from your children and the app is 100% offline. It is a peaceful, secure space where families can play with complete peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Kidamame Discovery have ads?

No. Kidamame Discovery is 100% ad-free. We believe children should be able to explore and learn in a calm environment without interruptions, as advertisements ruin the quality and the flow of the play experience.

Do I need a subscription?

No subscriptions. We offer a simple, transparent pricing model. You can download and try the app for free to see if it's right for your family. If you love it, a single one-time purchase unlocks the entire world of wonder forever.

Is the app completely offline?

Yes! An internet connection is only needed at the time of purchase if you decide to unlock the full app. After that, Kidamame Discovery does not require an internet connection to play. It's truly offline, meaning no tracking, no data collection, and it's perfect for road trips or flights.

How many hours of exploration are there?

Depending on the child's pace, playtime can range from 30 minutes if they just explore and listen to the relaxing sounds, up to 60 minutes or more if they discover and read all the interesting facts with you.

Are there puzzles to solve or levels to unlock?

No. Kidamame is pure exploration and discovery in a Montessori style. It is designed to inspire curiosity about our world and nature without any stressful goals, timers, or scoreboards.

Do you plan to add more biomes or scenes?

We might add one or two scenes, but it's not guaranteed. Because our app is completely offline and all content is included in your download, we have strict storage limits to keep the app size manageable. It's possible we will release new biomes as separate "chapters" in new apps. Sign up for our newsletter below to stay updated on future releases!

What is the recommended age for the app?

We believe children under 4 should not use screens. Kidamame Discovery is designed for kids ages 4 and up, but it grows with them! Younger kids can explore with a parent who can read the facts to them, while older children can delve into the details on their own. Actually, thanks to the fascinating nature facts and the calm, relaxing atmosphere, the app is genuinely engaging for adults too!
