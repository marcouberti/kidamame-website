Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Kidamame uses them on our website, and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

Currently, Kidamame is committed to providing a clean, privacy-first experience. Our website (kidamame.com) uses minimal cookies. We do not use intrusive advertising or profiling cookies.

When you visit our website, you may encounter a cookie consent banner (provided by Cookiebot). We use this to respect your tracking preferences. By default, no non-essential cookies are loaded unless requested by third-party embedded content or unless you explicitly give your consent.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are cookies that are required for the operation of our website. They include, for example, cookies that enable basic functions like page navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use basic, anonymized analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website to improve our web content. These do not track personal data.

4. Our Educational Apps

Our educational apps designed for children (such as Kidamame Discovery) are entirely offline and ad-free once downloaded. The apps do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

5. Managing Your Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through the cookie consent banner on our website or by adjusting the settings in your web browser. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at info@kidamame.com.
